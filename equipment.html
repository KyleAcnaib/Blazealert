Essential Safety Equipment

Must-have safety equipment for every household

Smoke Detectors

Early warning system for fire detection and family protection.

  • Types: Ionization, Photoelectric, Dual-sensor
  • Placement: Every level, near bedrooms, hallways
  • Maintenance: Test monthly, replace batteries annually
  • Lifespan: Replace entire unit every 10 years
  • Features: Interconnected, voice alerts, smart connectivity

Fire Extinguishers

First line of defense against small fires before they spread.

  • Class A: Ordinary combustibles (wood, paper)
  • Class B: Flammable liquids (gasoline, oil)
  • Class C: Electrical equipment
  • Class K: Cooking oils and fats
  • ABC Type: Multi-purpose for home use
  • Maintenance: Check pressure monthly, service annually

Carbon Monoxide Detectors

Detect deadly carbon monoxide gas from faulty appliances.

  • Placement: Near gas appliances, bedrooms
  • Types: Battery, hardwired, plug-in
  • Testing: Monthly button test
  • Replacement: Every 5-7 years
  • Features: Digital display, peak level memory
  • Installation: Wall or ceiling mount

Gas Leak Detectors

Specialized detectors for natural gas and LPG leaks.

  • Types: Natural gas, LPG, combination units
  • Features: Audio/visual alarms, automatic shut-off
  • Placement: Near gas appliances and connections
  • Power: Battery backup for power outages
  • Sensitivity: Adjustable detection levels
  • Maintenance: Regular calibration and testing
